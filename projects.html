MNIST Latent Space

Explore the wonderful world of the MNIST latent space. The MNIST dataset is an open-source colllection of 60,000 numbers. I trained a neural network on the dataset, and then by using what it learned, you can explore the space "in between" different numbers. It's sort of hard to explain - just click on this experiment and you'll see.

Sentiment Analysis AI

This LSTM was trained on a dataset of hundreds of thousands of tweets (Sentiment140 Dataset). From these tweets, it learned to detect sentiment and tone of short messages. The LSTM will give a number between -1 (very negative) and 1 (very positive). Go try it out now!

The Mystical Field

This was a small project I created while learning some three.js. I downloaded a fox model and some plants from clara.io's library, and then plopped them into a scene. From there, I made the plants randomly generate, made the fox controllable, and added some fog. End result: a "magestic" garden that you can explore. Running this is performance intensive, so it may not work on a potato pc.
